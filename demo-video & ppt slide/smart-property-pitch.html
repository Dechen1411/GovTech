Problem 02 / 07

Property is high trust, but the process is still heavy.

  • Citizens face repeated office visits and paper submissions.
  • Buyers need stronger proof that documents are real.
  • Government officers need a cleaner review and audit trail.
  • Full property purchase is too expensive for many citizens.
  • Lease and usage rights are hard to track clearly.
Manual verification Limited access to ownership
Solution 03 / 07

One verified account for property services.

A citizen does not choose buyer or seller. One NDI verified account can submit, buy, resell, and lease.

Identity Login starts with Bhutan NDI verification.
Wallet The platform creates a secure wallet so users do not manage private keys.
Review Government officers approve property documents before minting shares.
Market Users buy full or fractional ownership and record leases.
NDI first Citizen friendly blockchain UX
Trust Model 04 / 07

Built for privacy and auditability.

NDI verified users Every major action begins from a verified identity session.
Encrypted documents Sensitive legal files stay off-chain and encrypted before storage.
SHA-256 hashes Document integrity can be checked without exposing the document publicly.
Admin audit trail Approvals, purchases, and lease events are tracked for review.
On-chain balances Shares and lease events can be verified from the contract state.
Production controls Officer identities, role allowlists, and security audits come before launch.
Privacy by design Audit-friendly workflows
